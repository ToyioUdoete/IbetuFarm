Who We Are

Rooted in Tradition, Growing for the Future Nestled amidst the fertile embrace of Akwa Ibom's Ukat Nsịt, Ibetu Farm thrives on a heritage woven from respect for the land and a passion for quality produce. For the past nine years, under the visionary leadership of Chairman Elder Obot Udoete, we have nurtured more than just crops and livestock; we have cultivated a legacy of tradition, innovation, and community.

We believe in fair trade practices, ensuring everyone along the chain, from our dedicated farmers to our valued customers, benefits from our success.

Join Us on Our Journey Each bite of our produce tells a story – a story of tradition, innovation, and a deep commitment to quality. We invite you to experience the difference. Explore our farm, savor the freshness, and connect with the passion that drives us. Together, let's cultivate a future where sustainable practices, quality food, and community spirit go hand in hand.
